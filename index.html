Quick Bio

Dr John Oosthuizen holds a PhD (Music) from Stellenbosch University (2025). His research focuses on organ performance practice, South African jazz idioms and the intersection of improvisation and liturgical repertoire. John teaches aural training, music theory and keyboard skills and serves as Music Director at Stellenbosch Catholic Church.

  • PhD in Music (Stellenbosch University, 2023–2025)
  • MMus Organ Performance (2020–2022)
  • School choir conductor & private piano tutor

Profile

Academic, instructional, and technical writer and editor with experience producing theses, courses, and technical publications. Skilled in synthesizing complex concepts and creating structured long-form content. Active as a university music lecturer, researcher, private educator, and church music director.

Research focuses include jazz-inflected organ aesthetics, South African jazz idioms, French Romantic organ traditions, and Gregorian chant. Experienced in primary, secondary, and tertiary music curriculum design.

Testimonials available on request.

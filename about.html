About Dr John Oosthuizen

Profile

Academic, instructional and technical writer and editor; university music lecturer and school educator with deep experience in curriculum design and performance practice.

Education

  • PhD in Music (Stellenbosch University, 2023–2025)
  • MMus (Organ repertoire interpretation & performance practice), Stellenbosch University (2020–2022)
  • MMus coursework (Theory & Composition), UNC Greensboro, North Carolina, USA (2020)
  • BMus Honours (cum laude), Stellenbosch University (2019)
  • Mini-MBA, Harvard Business School, Boston, MA (2010)
  • BSc Mathematics & Computer Science — UKZN (1978)

Selected Experience & Roles

Stellenbosch University — Music Lecturer (Aural Training & Music Theory). Music Director — Stellenbosch Catholic Church. Private piano teacher; choir conductor with multiple eisteddfod awards.

Publications

Technical publications for IBM and McGraw Hill; recent academic outputs on organ repertoire and South African jazz aesthetics.
